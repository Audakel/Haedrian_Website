Curo is a secure, fast and easy-to-use mobile wallet for Microfinance Institutions

Perfect to issue and repay microloans in your local currency with a text or android phone

  • Secure

    Multi-signature, multi-factor authentication, and real-time monitoring of wallets. You'll have the most secure app around without needing to know a thing about the latest security features.

  • Simple

    Easy to load wallet balance, easy to send. Using our network of exchange points to deposit and withdraw cash means no bank account is ever required.

  • Sync

    Open API allows it to interface with your loan management software and automatically update loan repayments / disbursements in realtime.
