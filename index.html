We imagine a world where all people, no matter how remote, are connected through mobile technology; enabling them to create opportunity for themselves, as well as their families and community. We believe providing secure, simple, and affordable access to capital will achieve this.

About Haedrian

Companies like Western Union and MoneyGram are taking an average of 9% to 25% of that money in fees to support a bloated and antiquated business model.

Mobile Phone Growth

Why choose to launch on Android? It is ubiquitous, with 82.7% world mobile market share and with “smart” phones already available for less than $40 at retail. Also, Ericsson Mobility estimates that in the next 3-4 years there will be nearly 5.6 billion smartphone subscriptions in the world, with most growth in cell phone purchases fueled from developing nations.

Connectivity Growth

Smartphones are definitely becoming cheaper, but that’s not the sole reason for their global spread; data plans are becoming far more inexpensive. Dozens of companies such as Facebook, Virgin Galatic, oneWeb have seen the potential growth a connected world would offer their company. Much attention has been focused on Elon Musk’s SpaceX raising $1 billion in funding from Google and Fidelity to fund development and launch of 700 extremely low orbit mini-satellites; eventual target is 4,000 satellites - which will give very low cost/ high-speed Internet access to the much of the world.

Only about 40% of the world, or 3.05 billion people have access to the internet. What does the world look like when that number doubles?

Bitcoin Protocol

What is Bitcoin? It's a distributed peer-to-peer, highly cryptographical, digital currency; it's like electronic cash you can use to pay friends or merchants, but levels of magnitude more efficient, cheaper, and secure than current systems. The protocol assures that not only is every single transaction that ever takes place, recorded, but also verified by all users.
